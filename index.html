About PATHAK BIOFUELS

Our Manufacturing Strength

PATHAK BIOFUELS is one of India’s fastest growing biofuel manufacturing and supply companies. Our manufacturing units are located in Panna and Kanpur.

We operate through our own manufacturing units along with 15+ partner units across India. Our production capability is around 160 Tons Per Day.

We specialize in industrial-grade biomass fuel solutions for thermal power plants, industrial boilers, textile industries, paper industries and large-scale industrial fuel applications.

What Makes Us Different

Our biggest strength is uninterrupted supply and consistent quality.

During monsoon season many industries face fuel shutdown due to high moisture in biomass fuel, but PATHAK BIOFUELS maintains stable supply throughout the year.

We focus on long-term industrial relationships, fast dispatch, quality control and maintaining supply consistency throughout the year.
